What clubs/activities/events have you enjoyed this year?

Semantic Map

This map visualizes student responses regarding the clubs, activities, or events they enjoyed this year. Each point represents a response, and responses closer together are semantically similar. Points are colored by cluster.

Cluster Summaries

Cluster 0 Summary (Extracurriculars & Social Interaction - Specific)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 23

Main Themes Identified: Extracurricular activities and social interaction; specifically, D&D (Dungeons & Dragons) and participation in clubs/teams (Green Team, Ultimate, Band).

Cluster 1 Summary (Extracurriculars & Social Engagement)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 25

Main Themes Identified: Extracurricular activities and social engagement; club participation and community building.

Cluster 2 Summary (Extracurricular Involvement, School Events, Desire for More)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 25

Main Themes Identified:

  1. Extracurricular Involvement & Social Connection: Students overwhelmingly enjoyed school clubs, sports teams, and social events, highlighting the importance of these activities for community building and friendship.
  2. School-Organized Activities & Events: Specific events like pep rallies, fundraisers, winter formal, karaoke, and lunch-time activities were cited, indicating enjoyment derived from school-sponsored entertainment and competitive opportunities.
  3. Desire for More Opportunities: A sub-theme emerges expressing a desire for a wider variety of clubs and activities and easier access to participation.

Cluster 3 Summary (Sports & Athletics)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 37

Main Themes Identified: Participation in and enjoyment of sports and athletic activities.

Cluster 4 Summary (Extracurriculars, Social Events, Community Engagement)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 39

Main Themes Identified: Extracurricular activities, social events, and community engagement.
