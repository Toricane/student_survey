School Event Suggestions

Semantic Map

This map visualizes student responses regarding suggestions for school events. Each point represents a response, and responses closer together are semantically similar. Points are colored by cluster.

Cluster Summaries

Cluster 0 Summary (Social & Community Events)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 28

Main Themes Identified:

More frequent and varied social events, primarily focused on student interaction and community building within the school. This includes both informal gatherings (lounge events, movie nights) and organized competitions/activities (tournaments, spirit weeks). Increased opportunities for cross-grade interaction are also desired.

Cluster 1 Summary (Indifference & Desire for More Activities)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 22

Main Themes Identified:

Indifference/Lack of Ideas and Desire for More Extracurricular Activities.

Cluster 2 Summary (Extracurricular & Interactive Events)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 37

Main Themes Identified:

Increased extracurricular activities, specifically field trips and events incorporating food, performance arts, and friendly competition. A desire for more engaging, interactive experiences beyond traditional classroom learning.

Cluster 3 Summary (Competitive, Performance & Social Events)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 24

Main Themes Identified:

Competitive events (sports, gaming, academic), performances (music, talent shows), and social gatherings (parties, dances).

Cluster 4 Summary (Larger-Scale, Career & Food Events)

Number of Responses in Cluster: 15

Main Themes Identified:

Desire for larger-scale events, career-focused activities, and food-related events.
